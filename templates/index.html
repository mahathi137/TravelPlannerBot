Essential Travel Tips

📋
Plan Ahead

Book accommodation and transport 2–4 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (Oct–Feb) and long weekends.

🛡️
Travel Insurance

Always buy travel insurance covering medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost luggage — especially for international travel.

💳
Smart Budgeting

Keep a daily budget log, carry cash for rural areas, and maintain a 20% emergency buffer. UPI works widely across India now.

🌦️
Check the Weather

Research destination climate before packing. India has extreme diversity — from Himalayan snowfall to tropical heat and monsoons.

🎒
Pack Smart

Pack light — roll clothes to save space. Always carry: power bank, universal adapter, first-aid kit, and copies of all documents.

🤝
Respect Local Culture

Learn a few words of the local language, dress modestly at religious sites, and always ask permission before photographing people.
